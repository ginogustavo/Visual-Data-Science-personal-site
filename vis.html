Mortality rates for leading causes of death in every U.S. county from 1980 to 2014.

This graph is found in a article on fivethirtyeight.com titled "35 Years Of American Death". I find this graph interesting because it displays bunch of information in a single chart, it shows not only the top cause of death in the last decades but numbers in each end of the study plust a trend where we can appreciated how drastic was the rise. I acknowledge the most tough work was on collecting the data but this visualization also attemps foster the awareness within the population about this matter, even to the less educated people.
